SPECIAL DISCOUNT PROGRAMS

Automobile & Homeowners Insurance All benefited employees are eligible for group discounts offered through MetLife® for automobile and homeowners insurance through payroll deduction. For more information contact MetLife http://www.metlife.com/mybenefits, or call 800-438-6381. (Company name: University of Massachusetts).

Group Legal Plan All benefited employees are eligible for the MetLife® Hyatt Legal Plan. This program offers representation for legal matters such as real estate, preparing wills and trusts and identity theft. Please call 800-821-6400 for more information.
